About Me

Hello! My name is Harry Furusho and welcome to my portfolio site. Here you will find a page of projects that I've worked on, as well as how to contact me.

A bit about myself: I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor's of Science in applied physics. When I graduated, I was unsure if I wanted to have a career in physics and so I quickly got a job as a legal assistant through a roommate who had majored in political science. This was my first job and was a huge lesson in what kind of career I wanted. While law is interesting, I figured it wasn't for me. I then thought back to some of my favorite classes back in my undergrad and remembered how challenging but fun my programming classes were. So that brings things to now, where I am trying to further my knowledge of web development and make a career of it.

In my spare time, I enjoy going for runs, longboarding, exploring the Pacific Northwest, or just hanging out with my friends and family.
